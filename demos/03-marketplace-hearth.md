# Hearth — The Home Chef Marketplace

## Overview
Hearth is a two-sided marketplace connecting home cooks with diners who want restaurant-quality meals at half the price. Home cooks set up a profile, list weekly menus, and prep dishes from their certified home kitchen. Diners browse by cuisine, neighborhood, and delivery window, order, and either pick up or get delivery via our driver network. We take a 22% platform fee.

## The opportunity
Home cooking is a massive untapped supply. Every neighborhood has 5-10 people who cook incredible food for family gatherings but have no way to monetize that skill. On the demand side, Gen Z and millennials increasingly want authentic, non-chain food at a price that isn't $35/entree. The gap between these two groups is the business.

## Launch market
San Francisco. We've picked SF because: (1) high density of immigrant home cooks with untapped culinary skill, (2) high willingness to pay among tech workers, (3) the Bay Area's "cottage food law" (AB 626) made selling home-cooked meals legal in 2019 — California is the only state where this is fully legal at scale.

## Product
Mobile app (iOS + Android). Diners browse, book, rate. Cooks upload menus, manage orders, set prep schedules. We handle payments, insurance (we've partnered with Next Insurance), and delivery logistics through a network of gig drivers paid at $1.50/delivery + tips.

## Pricing
Diners pay cost + 22% platform fee + $2.99 delivery. Home cooks keep 78% of the menu price. Average order value is projected at $18.

## Growth plan
- **Month 1-3**: Recruit 50 home cooks in the Mission + SOMA. We have a "founding cook" program — $500 stipend + free professional food photography.
- **Month 4-6**: Open app to diners. Target 5,000 diners in first 60 days.
- **Month 7-12**: Expand to Oakland + South Bay, sign up 300+ cooks, 50K diners.
- **Month 13+**: Expand to LA, Seattle, Portland (other states with emerging cottage food laws).

## Defensibility
Network effects. Once we have liquid supply in a neighborhood, the next marketplace can't easily displace us without subsidizing the same dual-sided ramp. Our "founding cook" program creates a cohort of loyal cooks with multi-year relationships.

## Unit economics (year 1)
Average order: $18. Our take: $3.96. Delivery cost: $3. Payment processing: $0.80. Contribution margin per order: $0.16. We lose money on year 1, profitable by year 2 at scale.
