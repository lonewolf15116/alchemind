# Axle — AI Sales Coach for Mid-Market AEs

## Overview
Axle is an AI-powered sales coach for B2B account executives. It listens to live sales calls (via Zoom/Meet/Gong integrations), and after each call delivers a 2-minute personalized coaching digest: what worked, what didn't, and the three questions the rep should have asked but didn't. We target mid-market SaaS companies (200-2,000 employees) where AEs run 15-30 calls a week and first-line managers are stretched thin.

## Why now
Every AE we've talked to said the same thing: "My manager listens to maybe one call a month. I have no idea if I'm getting better." Call intelligence tools exist (Gong, Chorus) but they're pipeline visibility tools for managers — not coaching tools for reps. The market is wide open for a rep-first product, and the latest generation of multimodal models can finally do this reliably.

## Target customer
Mid-market SaaS with 10-50 AEs, ACV $20K-$200K, inside sales model. Typical decision maker: VP Sales or Head of Sales Enablement. Our ideal design partner has 25 AEs, each making $100K OTE, and a complex product that newer reps struggle to pitch.

## Pricing
$99/rep/month, annual contracts. At 25 AEs that's $29,700/yr per customer. We've targeted this price because it's below the threshold that typically requires procurement sign-off for a "productivity tool," which lets us sell directly to the VP Sales.

## Go-to-market

- Warm intros through our advisors (ex-Gong, ex-Outreach execs)
- Content: a weekly newsletter called "The Coaching Gap" targeting Heads of Sales Enablement
- Conference presence at SaaStr and Dreamforce in year 2

## Defensibility
Our advantage is product velocity. We're shipping a week-over-week improving product on top of foundation models, and we have proprietary call data from our design partners that improves our coaching quality over time. Long term, we build a proprietary sales skills taxonomy that becomes the data moat.
